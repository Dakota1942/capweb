Keeping You & Your Devices Safe

Security awareness is used in organizations to manage the level of human risk. It's important to be aware of threats and how they affect our university. Human error could be as simple as someone clicking on a bad link in an email and entering credentials. This could give a hacker the ability to gain access to sensitive information. Taking a little time to learn about security threats and how to look out for sketchy emails, bad websites, how to create proper passwords, etc. are great ways to reduce risk to our university.
