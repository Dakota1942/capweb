Keeping You & Your Devices Safe

Security awareness is used in organizations to educate people on cybersecurity and reduce some of the risk that come from accidents or people that have bad intentions. It is important to be aware of threats and how they could affect you and others at the university. An accident could be as simple as someone clicking on a bad link in an email and entering their credentials when it asks. This could give a hacker the ability to gain access to sensitive information. Taking a little time to learn about security threats and how to reduce risk, will help to reduce risk to everyone connected to the university.
